Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To convert used materials into valuable products and support sustainable micro-economies.

Vision: A world where household waste is viewed as a resource, empowering local communities through skill-building and green entrepreneurship.

Why We Started

We saw the growing issue of plastic waste in our communities and realized that with the right platform, this waste could become raw material for local artists. RECYCLY was born out of the desire to create an accessible, transparent pipeline from the recycling bin to the artisan's workbench.

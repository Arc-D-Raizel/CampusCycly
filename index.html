Welcome to RECYCLY 🌱

We connect households, micro-artisans, and consumers to transform plastic waste into valuable handmade products. Join us in creating a sustainable, circular economy right in your local community.

How It Works: System Flow

Households → RECYCLY Platform → Micro-Artisans → Consumers

  • Households: Collect and sort usable recyclables.
  • RECYCLY Platform: Bridges the gap with real-time tracking and transactions.
  • Micro-Artisans: Craft beautiful, sustainable goods.
  • Consumers: Purchase eco-friendly items with a transparent history.

Visual Workflow

Phase 1: Collection

Household users clean, dry, and weigh their PET bottles. They log these materials onto the RECYCLY platform, creating a digital inventory of available raw materials in their area.

Phase 2: Distribution

The RECYCLY platform acts as the intermediary. It notifies local crafters when materials are available, handling the logistics of raw material purchasing and transfer securely.

Phase 3: Creation & Sales

Micro-artisans receive the raw materials and use our tutorials to create beautiful, upcycled products. These are then listed back on the RECYCLY Market for consumers to purchase, completing the loop.
